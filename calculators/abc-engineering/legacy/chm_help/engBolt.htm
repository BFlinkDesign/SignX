How to use the dialog window "Anchor Bolt Base Plate"

This dialog window is shown in Figure 1. It allows to calculate average centroid height for multiple sign cabinets.

If any term that is used in this dialog box is unknown to you, please refer to glossary of terms.

Note: All base plates must be fully grouted after pipes have been installed.

For getting results, enter your data to each edit box, then click on the Calculate button. After a few seconds (depends on your machine speed) the results will be displayed.

Note the difference between "Desired Bolt diameter" and "Accepted Bolt diameter". The first value is entered by user, and the second is calculated by application. If your desired bolt diameter is too small, new and larger bolt diameter shall be selected and displayed as "Accepted bolt diameter". If you don’t know what should be desired bolt diameter, leave it as zero. Application shall choose the smallest diameter that stands the requirements.

Note that the largest bolt diameter can not exceed 3 inches. If you try to exceed this limit you are warned. No results will be generated.

If your requirements are too severe, application may fail to find a solution. You will be warned about this. In this case you shall need a special engineering.

For proper interpreting of recommended pipe sizes refer to "Calculating perimeter bending force" article.

Results and information about each sign on list may be printed. For this, click on the button Print Summary. Printer setup dialog window shall appear, where you can select appropriate printer. Then click OK, and here you are.

For your convenience all entered information and calculated results may be removed from dialog window. For this, click on the button Clear, and controls in dialog window shall be reset to appropriate default values.
