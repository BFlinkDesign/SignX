How to use the dialog window "Avg.Centroid Ht"

This dialog window is shown in Figure 1. It allows to calculate average centroid height for multiple sign cabinets.

If any term that is used in this dialog box is unknown to you, please refer to glossary of terms.

For getting results, fill in the "List of Entered Signs" and click on the button Calculate. List of signs is appended by entering new sign cabinet area and its centroid height to edit boxes "Sign Area" and "Centroid Height", then click on the button Add New. New sign cabinet shall be appended to list end.

If you wish to edit information about any sign on list, then click with your mouse left button on appropriate sign data in list, then click on the button Delete. This removes sign from list for editing in edit boxes "Sign Area" and "Centroid Height". After you finish editing, click on the Add New button to add this sign to list again.

If you wish just to remove any sign from list, then click it with your mouse left button in sign list. Then click on the button Delete. Sign shall be removed.

Results and information about each sign on list may be printed. For this, click on the button Print Summary. Printer setup dialog window shall appear, where you can select appropriate printer. Then click OK, and here you are.

For your convenience all entered information and calculated results may be removed from dialog window. For this, click on the button Clear All, and controls in dialog window shall be reset to appropriate default values.
