Base Plate to Column Connections with Gussets
Table is only for schedule 40 pipe
Note: use anchor bolt program to determine bolt sizes, location and baseplate dimensions
        (minimums with gussets are shown below.  Use larger of the two mehtods of sizing baseplates)
         When spacing anchor bolts, allow for nut clearance relative to fillet welds
Note: Column connections without gussets are approximately 70% of the strength of the column section modulus 
         when welded with fillet welds equal to the column wall thickness.
Assumptions:         Fillet welds are equal to column thickness,
Gussets are required to develop column section modulus at base plate and are at 4 places 90 degrees apart
Base plate thickness is at least 2 times column thickness
Gussets are to be welded all around and are 2 times the thickness  of the column min.
Effective throat of fillet weld is .707 times the fillet weld size
The 'toes' of the gusset shall be removed to the size of the fillet weld.
The leg dimension is from the 90 degree angle to the ends after removing the material for the fillet weld.
