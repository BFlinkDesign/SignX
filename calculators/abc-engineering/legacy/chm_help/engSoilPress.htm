Depending on your soil type, you may need different foundation sizes. For suggesting proper foundation size, application needs to know vertical bearing soil pressure and lateral bearing soil pressure. Information below should help you to select correct soil pressure values.

 

Choosing correct soil type

Excerpt from Unified Build Code (UBC):

Clays and Silt

< 0.075 (difficult to observe without microscopic lenses.)

NOTE: Organic soils and Peat require foundation investigations.
