Description of terms used

Pipe section modulus is required to resist bending at the foundation. Whenever openings for electrical are constructed in the pipe, the openings shall be at 90 degrees to the wind direction, and reinforced as necessary to maintain pipe integrity.
Perimeter bending force is an engineering term which expresses the force of the tension on a flexible sign face, combined with wind force on the face, which is then transmitted to the frame as a bending force; expressed in pounds per foot of frame around the perimeter of sign.

How to use the dialog window "Perim. Bending Force"

This dialog window is shown in Figure 1.

If any term that is used in this dialog box is unknown to you, please refer to glossary of terms.

Enter information into edit boxes about your sign, and click on the button Calculate to get the results. They are showed in the rightmost window under title Results.

Note that for getting results all edit boxes must be filled.

Results and initial information about sign may be printed. For this, click on the button Print Summary. Printer setup dialog window shall appear, where you can select appropriate printer. Then click OK, and here you are.

For your convenience all entered information and calculated results may be removed from dialog window. For this, click on the button Clear, and controls in dialog window shall be reset to appropriate default values.

Results display:

  • The data that was used for calculation (centroid height, sign area, number of support columns, wind pressure, perimeter of sign, steel type).
  • Calculated values (section modulus, recommended to install pipe sizes , perimeter bending force). Application commonly displays two recommended pipe sizes- one size is for round tubes, other- for square. This means that both sizes fit your application, and can be interchanged safely. Sometimes, especially for larger loading, square tube won’t be listed as recommendation for install. This means that it is not strong enough to handle loading forces.

In some cases, when your requirements results in a very large section modulus, application shall respond that it can not calculate a result. This means that you may require a special engineering for your sign.
