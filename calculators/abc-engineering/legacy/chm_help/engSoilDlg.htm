How to use the dialog window " Changing default soil pressure "

This dialog window is shown in Figure 1. It allows to select for calculations or customize soil pressure values that are stored on your system.

If any term that is used in this dialog box is unknown to you, please refer to glossary of terms.

For selecting default soil pressure type that is used in calculations, click on appropriate radio button, then click on the Choose button. If you wish to get some help on determining your soil type, click on the Explain button. This action shall display "Understanding soil pressure" article.

If existing soil pressure values do not satisfy your needs, you may add two custom values ("Custom1" and "Custom2") or edit predefined values. For adding custom values, just enter them and click on the Choose button. For editing predefined values, click on the button Edit All. This action shall enable by default disabled edit boxes and you will be able to enter your own values. For commiting changes click on the Choose button.

For abandon of any change in this dialog window (except for pressing "Restore Defaults" button) click on the Cancel button.

If you wish to restore predefined soil pressure values, just click on the "Restore Defaults" button. Note: all your custom values will be lost!
