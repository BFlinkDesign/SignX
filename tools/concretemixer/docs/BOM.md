## Sourcing Notes

### Steel Materials
- Steel tubing: Local steel supplier or metal supermarket
- Sheet steel: Same, or online metals retailers
- Auger flight: Custom fabrication or screw conveyor suppliers

### Motor & Electrical
- DC gear motor: Surplus Center, Amazon, Grainger
- Transformer: Electronics suppliers
- Switches: Electrical supply

### Wheels
- Marathon flat-free: Hardware stores, Amazon
- Axle materials: Steel suppliers

### Water System
- Brass fittings: Hardware stores, plumbing supply
- Spray nozzles: Agricultural supply, McMaster-Carr
